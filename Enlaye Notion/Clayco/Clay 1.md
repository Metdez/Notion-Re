# B2B Business-Development Intelligence Dossier: Clayco (claycorp.com)

## ⚠️ CRITICAL IDENTITY FINDING (read first)
The website **https://claycorp.com belongs to Clayco** (legal/operating entity *Clayco Inc.* / *Clayco Construction Company, Inc.*, under the *Clayco Enterprise* holding structure) — a national **design-build construction firm headquartered in Chicago, IL**, with its largest operations hub in **Berkeley/St. Louis, MO**. **It is NOT a Stoughton, Massachusetts mechanical/HVAC/plumbing contractor.** The brief's "Stoughton, MA" descriptor appears to conflate this firm with the unrelated **Clay Corporation** auto-dealer group (Clay Subaru/Nissan, Norwood/Stoughton, MA) and possibly with **Michael Clay Corporation** (a Nevada GC). Because the website is the authoritative identifier and Enlaye sells risk-management software to *large contractors*, this dossier covers **Clayco** — which is a far better-fit prospect than any local auto dealer. This identity conflict is logged in `gaps`.

## TL;DR
- **Clayco is a top-tier US design-build contractor** — ranked **#4 on ENR's Top 100 Design-Build Firms**, ~**No. 42 on the ENR Top 400 Contractors** list (by construction revenue), privately owned, founded **1984** by Bob Clark, ~**$8.1B self-reported total 2025 revenue** (≈$7.5B in 2024 per third-party reporting), **4,000+ employees**, HQ in **Chicago**.
- **Clayco does NOT operate as a set of regional GC "divisions."** It runs an integrated **"Clayco Enterprise"** parent over **sister/subsidiary companies** (CRG, Lamar Johnson Collaborative, Concrete Strategies, Ventana, Consolidated Distribution) **plus ~9–10 market-sector "Business Units"** (Industrial, Corporate, Institutional, Residential, Aviation, Science & Technology, Clayco Compute/data centers, Clayco Power & Energy, Government) and **regional offices** (Southwest/Phoenix, St. Louis, Chicagoland, West Coast). Projects map to these *business units + subsidiaries*, not geographic GC divisions.
- **Incumbent tech stack is mature and Autodesk-centric** — Autodesk Construction Cloud / Autodesk Build / Forma Build (migrated off BIM 360 + PlanGrid), Revit, DESTINI Estimator, Power BI, 4D BIM/VDC and drone reality capture — meaning Clayco is a sophisticated technology buyer that already invests in field/PM/estimating platforms but shows **no public evidence of a dedicated construction risk-management point solution** — a clear opening for Enlaye.

## Key Findings

**Firmographics.** Clayco was formed July 23, 1984 by Bob Clark (then 25). Headquarters moved to Chicago's Loop (35 E. Wacker Dr.) in 2013; the 230,000-sq-ft Berkeley, MO office (8640 Evans Ave.) is the operations hub. Self-reported **$8.1B+ revenue for 2025** and **4,000+ employees** (company overview page). Independent reporting put 2024 revenue at roughly **$7.5B** (Colliers/Crain's, ~29% YoY growth) and 2023 at **$5.3B** (Construction Dive) — so the $8.1B 2025 figure is the firm's own and is directionally consistent with rapid growth. Ownership is **private** (one of the largest privately owned AEC firms in the US).

**Leadership.** **Anthony Johnson** became **CEO in January 2025** (a 17-year veteran who previously led the Industrial Business Unit), succeeding **Russ Burns** (retired Dec. 31, 2024; died March 2025). **Bob Clark** remains **Executive Chairman**. Other key executives: **Steve Sieckhaus** (Enterprise President/COO), **Shawn Clark** (Chief Strategy Officer; CEO of CRG), **Kevin McKenna** (Chief Administrative Officer), **Mike Pierle** (COO, Construction), **Justin Lienemann** (CFO), **Carmen Hernandez** (EVP/General Counsel), **Jeff Miller** (CIO), **Michael Fassnacht** (Chief Growth Officer / President, Chicagoland), **Ryan McGuire** (President, Clayco Compute), **Zackery House** (CEO, Architecture & Engineering / LJC).

**Corporate structure (the core mechanic).** Clayco is a **single integrated enterprise**, not a multi-division GC. "Clayco Enterprise" (created 2019) is the parent over the **"Clayco Collective"**: the construction company (Clayco), **CRG** (real estate development/investment), **Lamar Johnson Collaborative** (architecture/engineering; merged with Clayco Design & Engineering in Jan. 2026), **Concrete Strategies** (self-perform concrete, >$200M/yr), **Ventana** (curtainwall/façade enclosure), and **Consolidated Distribution Company (CCDI)**. Within the construction company, work is organized by **market-sector Business Units**, the most relevant being Industrial, Corporate, Institutional, Residential, Aviation, Science & Technology, **Clayco Compute** (data centers, launched Jan. 2025), and **Clayco Power & Energy** (solar/BESS EPC, launched March 2026).

**Data center surge.** Data centers drove **$3.6B of revenue in 2024 — "accounting for half of Clayco's total company revenue and more than doubling its contribution from $1.5 billion in 2023"** (Clayco PR Newswire, Jan. 15, 2025). CEO Anthony Johnson told Construction Dive Clayco is **"already a top five builder of data centers in the U.S., with 57 active projects nationwide with the largest hyperscale and tech companies,"** and anticipates data-center revenue **"to reach over $4.6 billion by 2026"** (the official release states $4.5B). Clayco **"has completed or is currently undertaking more than $12.7 billion in advanced technology projects."** Building Design + Construction ranks Clayco **the fifth-largest data center contractor** (per Colliers, citing Crain's, Jan. 2025).

**Safety posture.** Clayco runs the branded **"Clayco Safe" / "BEYOND" safety program**; ENR reports its **"BEYOND Safety program reduced Clayco's TRIR to 0.55 by 2025 versus an industry average of 2.4"** (the 2026 about-page header also cites a 0.39 EMR). This is directly relevant to Enlaye: Clayco already treats safety/risk as a branded differentiator.

**Technology stack.** Per an Autodesk University 2024 session, **"Clayco, Inc. transitioned to Autodesk Forma / Forma Build from BIM 360 and PlanGrid"** enterprise-wide — confirming Autodesk Construction Cloud / Autodesk Build / Revit as the core PM/BIM platform. AGCMO AECTechCon 2025 sessions presented by Clayco staff document a **4D BIM quantity-based progress-tracking workflow**, a **historical cost dashboard built on DESTINI Estimator + Power BI**, and **reality-capture/drone** workflows. Third-party vendor data (LeadIQ) lists AWS, MongoDB, Splunk, and Juniper Square in Clayco's broader tech stack.
