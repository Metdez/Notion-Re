## What he founded — Enlaye
- **What it does:** AI-native Risk Lifecycle Management platform. Instead of reviewing documents or schedules in isolation, it analyzes data across *all* aspects of a project — using customer data plus a matrix of similar past projects — to categorize and **predict risks** like delays and construction errors ([ENR](https://www.enr.com/articles/63134-ai-powered-construction-risk-analysis-platform-enlaye-raises-5-million)).
- **Origin story:** Founded 2023 at the intersection of a lifelong passion for the built environment and a decade-long belief in human-inspired AI ([Enlaye](https://www.enlaye.com/who-we-are)).
- **Cofounder:** **Stamatios Liapis, PhD** (CTO) — AI & computational neuroscience expert; PhD Computational Neuroscience, Boston University School of Medicine; undergrad University of Pennsylvania. The two met in **high school near Saint-Germain-en-Laye, France (2009)**, reconnected in Boston in 2021, and started Enlaye after Philippe's MBA and Stamati's PhD ([Enlaye](https://www.enlaye.com/who-we-are)).
